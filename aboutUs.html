A stationery shop is a retail store specializing in products like paper, pens, notebooks, and other
office supplies. It often stocks items for school, work, and art, catering to students,
professionals, and hobbyists alike. Common products include pencils, markers, binders,
planners, and sometimes specialty items like fountain pens or high-quality
sketchbooks. Many stationery shops also offer gift cards, greeting cards, and
wrapping materials, making them a go-to for personalized gifts and creative
supplies. Modern shops may incorporate eco-friendly products, such as
recycled paper and refillable pens, appealing to environmentally conscious
customers. They provide a tactile, creative shopping experience.
